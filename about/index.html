About

Wanli Xue is a PhD candidate in Department of Computer Science and Engineer of the University of New South Wales. He is also a member of Networks Research Group of Data61 CSIRO, which used to be NICTA. He received the Master degree from the University of Tasmania. His current research interests as well as PhD topic are about Cyber-Physical Systems and private IoT.
